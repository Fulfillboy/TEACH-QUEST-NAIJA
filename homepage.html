The show

Our holistic approach is to reach the school-age children who remain excluded from education and see them through their journey to success thereby improving the social recognition and reward for their educators through reality show.

NexGen Leaders

The Teach Quest Naija show would is an Instructor-led show (ILS) show of fifteen 21st century leaders paired with 30 housed learners. This event is powered to support next-generation learners in Nigerian schools and out-of-school children; empowering them with the right knowledge, confidence and leadership intelligence they need to rise to the challenge of a changing world.

NexGen Learners

Teach Quest Naija targets transformative learners between the ages of 12 and 17 years who would be committed to develop their brand by committing to learning. The show would relish mentorships and remedial programs that addresses students learning gaps, thought-teaching and fun-packed activities designed to spark learners' strengths, talents, passion and curiosity for learning in the learners who wants to understand the world around them.

WHAT WE DO

Teach Quest Naija works towards a day when every child will receive an excellent education which would convey life-skill and rationale and would drive learners to think, dream, and explore their ideas in a supportive and positive environment.
We find and nurture leaders who will commit to imbue experimental education; turn lessons into project-based learning for development. We give priorities and respond to create the environment and experiences that encourages the learners' curiosities and inquiries to flourish.

Our Overall Goals

We aim to deliver to the learners the 21st-Century Learning Designs. And to produce effective learning that create efficient network of digital learners and leaders for the new normal.
